### Product Structure

The product consists of a client, management terminal, and server. The client is comprised of 4 functional modules:

1. Network testing module
2. Network information receiving/sending module
3. Human-machine interaction module
4. Training information collection module

The management terminal consists of a personal information recording module. The server consists of 3 functional modules:

1. Cloud service module
2. Training information analysis module
3. User information storage module

---

### Indications for Use

Used for visual function training and treatment of children with amblyopia (lazy eye).

---

### Contraindications

Use with caution in amblyopia patients with organic ophthalmic diseases.

---

### Precautions

1. **Train in a dark room.** Ensure there are no light sources in the room other than the computer screen.
2. **Maintain distance.** The trainee should sit **1.5 meters** away from the computer screen.
3. **Eye fatigue.** If visual fatigue occurs during use, take a short break. Resume training only after eye fatigue symptoms have subsided.
4. **Hardware and software requirements:**

   **Hardware:**
   - Desktop or laptop computer
   - Three-button mouse (left button, right button, and middle scroll wheel)
   - Internet connection
   - Audio/sound capability
   - Screen size: 15 inches or larger

   **Software:**
   - Windows 7 (64-bit) or later
   - Intel Core i3 2120 or higher
   - RAM: 4 GB or higher
   - Disk space: 120 GB or higher
   - Google Chrome browser (version 72 or later)

## Usage Instructions

### Before First Training

Please watch the instructional videos carefully before your first training session. RevitalVision training includes **4 training types**. Watch all 4 instructional videos in sequence and ensure you fully understand the training methods before beginning!

### Starting Training

Click **My Training** to begin. Training types alternate. Please respond carefully according to the corresponding response requirements by clicking the mouse.

---

### Training Modes

#### Single Image Training (单图训练)

The screen flashes **twice** — one flash contains a Gabor patch, the other does not.

- If the Gabor patch appears in the **first** flash → click **Left Mouse Button**
- If the Gabor patch appears in the **second** flash → click **Right Mouse Button**

| Left: Image with Gabor patch | Right: Image without Gabor patch |
|---|---|

---

#### Three Image Training (三图训练)

The screen flashes **twice** — one flash shows **3 Gabor patches**, the other shows only **2 Gabor patches**.

- If 3 patches appear in the **first** flash → click **Left Mouse Button**
- If 3 patches appear in the **second** flash → click **Right Mouse Button**

| Left: Image with 3 Gabor patches | Right: Image with 2 Gabor patches |
|---|---|

---

#### Clarity Training (清晰图像训练)

The screen flashes **twice** — both flashes contain a Gabor patch, but one is clearer and the other is fainter.

- If the **clearer** patch appears in the **first** flash → click **Left Mouse Button**
- If the **clearer** patch appears in the **second** flash → click **Right Mouse Button**

| Left: Clear Gabor patch image | Right: Blurry Gabor patch image |
|---|---|

---

#### Shift Training (移图训练)

The screen flashes **once**, showing **3 Gabor patches**. The middle patch may be shifted **up, down, left, or right** relative to its original position.

- If the middle patch is shifted **left** → click **Left Mouse Button**
- If the middle patch is shifted **right** → click **Right Mouse Button**
- For **up/down** shifts → rotate the mouse 90° counterclockwise:
  - If the patch is shifted **up** → click **Right Mouse Button**
  - If the patch is shifted **down** → click **Left Mouse Button**

*(Refer to the diagrams in the original manual for visual guidance.)*

---

### After Training

Once training is complete, the software displays the training results. Click **"Upload Training Results"** to submit.

### Next Training Session

After uploading training results, the server algorithm automatically adjusts the next training task based on the user's performance. The adjusted task is downloaded to the client computer when the user logs in for the next training session.
